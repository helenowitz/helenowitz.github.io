Privacy Policy Effective Date: September 25, 2025 ToDate (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application. Information Collection and Use ToDate does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with us or with third parties. The only data stored by the App is a list of dates selected by you for countdown purposes. These dates are saved locally on your device and are not accessible to us. Data Sharing and Disclosure Since no personal data is collected, no information is shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties. Data Retention and Control All data (your selected countdown dates) is stored only on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App. Children’s Privacy ToDate does not collect or store personal information from children or any users.
